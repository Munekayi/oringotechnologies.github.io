About ORINGO TECHNOLOGIES

Oringo is a technology company that aims to transform the way people work, play and communicate. We thrive to put ourselves in the shoes of not just our clients but of the communities of the world at large. Through innovation and great relationships with those in need of refreshing solutions to their problems we aim to develop a company so as to make products that solve crucial problems in societies around the world while having a special focus of African problems as well. We have a vision that seeks to ensure that Africans may have products they can call their own. This vision will be realized by having a range of products designed for every day African problems that will be produced and made available to the public.
